My name is Maria, like 90% of the Portuguese women!
I took a Bachelor’s degree in Biotechnology, but I came to the conclusion that it was not the right path for me and I’ve been redirecting my career since then.

I decided to enroll in a Bioinformatics Master’s with the goal of acquiring enough computer science skills to transition to an Informatics Engineering course, but I lost my scholarship and I had to find other ways to "make it".
That’s when I started to learn by myself. After several months of serious dedication (and 256 motivational quotes later), I landed my first job in tech, as a Frontend Developer!

Currently, I have solid knowledge in HTML, CSS and vanilla JavaScript ES6+. Professionally, I worked with TypeScript and Angular. My long term goal is to build end-to-end applications and become a Full Stack Developer.

Coding is my real passion. It is what motivates me to get up in the morning and what keeps me in a flow state during the day.
